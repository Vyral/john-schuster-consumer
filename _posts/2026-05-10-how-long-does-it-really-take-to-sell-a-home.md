How long will it take to sell your home? If that’s the first question you’re asking, you’re starting in the wrong place.

Most sellers focus on days on market. How fast did it go under contract? How quickly can I close? But the real timeline to sell a home doesn’t start the day the listing goes live. It starts weeks before that, with planning and positioning.

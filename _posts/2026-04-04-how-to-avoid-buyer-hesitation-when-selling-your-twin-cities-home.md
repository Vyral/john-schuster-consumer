Are buyers backing out or trying to renegotiate, and you’re not sure why?

Many sellers assume it is about price, but that is not what concerns buyers most. Here is what typically causes concern.

**What makes buyers hesitate?** It’s not the number on the listing that causes concern. It’s what they might find after they make an offer. Inspection issues, hidden repairs, and deferred maintenance create uncertainty.

When buyers feel that uncertainty, they either renegotiate more aggressively or walk away completely. That reaction isn’t always about the home’s price. It’s about avoiding unexpected problems.

**Why a prepared home matters.** A well-prepared home makes things easier, speeds up negotiations, and helps you keep more of your money. When buyers don’t see immediate issues, the process feels smoother and simpler for everyone.

Reducing uncertainty before listing your home makes it easier for buyers to feel comfortable buying the home.
